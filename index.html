Our Hybrid Solution

Our innovative system combines a Savonius wind turbine and Kaplan water turbine on a single floating platform, doubling energy output for coastal areas. It delivers uninterrupted power day and night, generating 363 joules (9x above target) with exceptional efficiency. The corrosion-resistant, low-maintenance design withstands harsh marine environments while reducing fossil fuel dependence. Eco-friendly and scalable, it supports Egypt's 2030 sustainability goals with zero emissions. This compact solution is ready for solar integration, offering affordable, reliable clean energy.
